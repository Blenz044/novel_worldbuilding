Itzli

Throughout the story Itzli will have a negative character arch. In the third novel this negative character arch will be completed as she kills her friend. Throughout books 4 and 5 she will have a redemption arch where she is ultimately killed in the climax.

Alana

Alana is a character that was initially going to be used in a different novel but I have chosen to include her in this one and focus on writing one novel at a time. She is a member of a barbarian tribe that is in the Midabour Peaks. They worship the dragon god Kyzeot. She will be a main character throughout the story.

Dorian

Dorian was also going to be in another book with Alana but I have chosen to merge those books into one. I do not know where he will fit into the story quite yet but I am thinking that he will still be from the military in some capacity.

Merchant's Son

This character does not have a name quite yet although he will probably be pivotal to world building throughout. He will be used to world build other parts of the world while the main characters are focused on other things.

Emperor

This emperor is also un-named so far but he was initially intended on being a part of the the novel with Dorian and Alana. He is supposed to be in charge of an empire that is reminiscent of the Roman Empire. He is also known for killing gods and is the one that slayed the dragon god Kyzeot. He has a group of soldiers that is based on the immortals from Persian history.
